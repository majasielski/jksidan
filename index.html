Artists Featuring In the Album:

The wait for details about Jungkook's highly-anticipated debut solo album has finally come to an end! On Oct. 3 — following the July release of his Billboard No. 1 single, "Seven," and his September follow-up single, "3D" — Big Hit Entertainment announced in a Weverse statement that Jungkook will release his first album called "Golden" on Nov. 3.

He released the lead single "Seven" featuring rapper Latto, which went on to top the US Billboard Hot 100.

Golden takes its motif from the "golden moments" of Jungkook as a solo artist. He is the "Golden Maknae" (youngest member) of BTS who is said to showcase "his unique timbre that will captivate listeners worldwide".

Jeon Jungkook, singer

"Golden" will include 11 tracks, including Jungkook's previously released tracks "Seven" and "3D."

Proof by Jungkook

Realise Date: June 10 2022

Artists featuring in the album:

  • Kim Namjoon
  • Kim Seokjin
  • Min Yoongi
  • Jung Heosok
  • Park Jimin

PROOF is an anthology album meant to reflect on BTS chronicle, each member musical taste, and the fans. It consists of previous tracks from the group’s nine years of discography. It also includes five new songs: the album's lead single "Yet to Come (The Most Beautiful Moment)", "Run BTS", "For Youth", and "애매한 사이" (Young Love).

The title is a reference to the Korean name of the group (Bangtan Sonyeondan / 방탄소년단), which translates to ‘Bulletproof Boyscouts.’

BTS' anthology album "Proof" sold over 2 million copies on the first day of its release.
